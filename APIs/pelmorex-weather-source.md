### Pelmorex Weather Source

Global weather and climate data APIs from Pelmorex — the Canadian company behind The Weather Network, MétéoMédia, Eltiempo, Clima, and Otempo — delivered through its OnPoint® platform, with an ongoing free tier and sales-quoted paid plans.

- **Based in:** Oakville, Ontario, Canada
- **Access model:** Mixed — Free plan is instant self-serve (sign up, get an API key); Starter / Professional / Business plans require sales contact (pricing quoted on request)
- **Pricing model:** Freemium (ongoing free tier; paid tiers custom / contact for pricing)
- **Coverage / data types:** hourly and daily forecasts (GFS, ECMWF global, HRRR rapid-refresh), historical (NOAA, from previous-day up to 20 years by tier), nowcast, NWS alerts, air quality forecast (CAMS), UV forecast, astronomy, climatology, plus lightning and pollen as priced add-ons; core parameters include temperature, precipitation, snowfall, wind, pressure, humidity, solar radiation, and cloud cover
- **Free tier:** $0/year — 1,000 credits/day; GFS forecast and previous-day NOAA historical only (credits are a per-request usage unit; consumption varies by endpoint/parameters)
- **Paid tiers:** Starter (5,000 credits/day, +HRRR/alerts/nowcast/UV/astronomy/location, 1-year history); Professional (150,000 credits/day, +ECMWF global/CAMS air quality/climatology, 5-year history); Business (from 625,000 credits/day, +elevation-adjusted products, 20-year history) — all custom / contact for pricing
- **Rate limits:** daily credit allotments (1,000 free → 625k+ on Business); no published per-minute limit
- **Output formats:** JSON and JSONP, in metric, imperial, or standard units; French and Spanish translations available
- **Geographic strength:** global — high-resolution grid covering all land areas and up to 200 miles offshore
- **Notable limitations:** paid-tier pricing isn't public (sales contact required); the free tier is narrow (GFS forecast + previous-day history only, no alerts/nowcast); lightning and pollen are separately quoted add-ons; "credits" usage unit can make cost estimation less transparent
- **Built on:** proprietary OnPoint® platform blending global models — NOAA GFS, ECMWF, NOAA HRRR, and Copernicus CAMS (air quality) — with NWS alert data
- **Docs:** https://developer.weathersourceapis.com/apis/
- **Last verified:** 2026-06
