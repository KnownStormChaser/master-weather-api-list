### Visual Crossing

Developer-friendly global weather API from Visual Crossing, unifying 50+ years of history, current conditions, 15-day forecasts, and climate normals behind a single Timeline endpoint — with a genuinely free 1,000-records/day tier, pay-as-you-go metering, and self-serve subscription plans.

- **Based in:** Reston, Virginia, USA
- **Access model:** Instant self-serve — free API key on sign-up; the Metered (pay-as-you-go), Professional, and Corporate data plans plus the Timeline LLX Core/Premium tiers are all self-serve ("Choose plan"); Enterprise and Timeline LLX Ultimate go through sales contact
- **Pricing model:** Freemium (ongoing 1,000 records/day free for commercial & non-commercial use; metered and subscription tiers above; Enterprise custom)
- **Coverage / data types:** single-call Timeline API unifying current conditions, 15-day forecast, 50+ years of history, historical forecasts, and climate normals/statistics at sub-hourly/hourly/daily resolution; ~100 elements — core (temperature, precipitation, wind, humidity, UV), solar & wind energy, agriculture (soil temperature/moisture, evapotranspiration, degree days), maritime (wave height, swell), air quality, astronomy, and severe-weather events (hail, tornado, wind damage, earthquakes) plus alerts; weather map tiles (radar, precipitation, lightning, temperature, etc.); separate Timeline LLX ultra-low-latency, high-concurrency API
- **Free tier:** ongoing — 1,000 weather records/day, free for commercial & non-commercial use, with full element/forecast/history access (a single 15-day forecast counts as one record; each history record counts individually); attribution required at the free level
- **Paid tiers:** *Data plans* — Metered $0.0001/record pay-as-you-go (unlimited records & concurrency); Professional $35/mo or $350/yr (10M records/mo, single concurrency, 1 user); Corporate $150/mo or $1,500/yr (unlimited records, team concurrency, adds energy/agriculture/maritime, degree days, OData, no attribution); Enterprise custom / contact (external sharing, invoicing, >99.9% SLA). *Timeline LLX* — Core $99/mo or $990/yr (50M queries/mo, 5,000/min); Premium $299/mo or $2,990/yr (200M queries/mo, 40,000/min); Ultimate custom / contact. *Add-ons* — Historical Forecast $165/mo or $1,450/yr; Loading Accelerator $600/mo; Weather Maps & Weather Statistics included
- **Rate limits:** Free 1,000 records/day; Professional 10M records/mo at 1 concurrent request (10,000 records & 100 locations per query); Metered/Corporate raise record, location, and concurrency caps (Corporate to 10 concurrent); Timeline LLX Core 5,000 queries/min, Premium 40,000/min; "unlimited" plans are governed by a fair-use policy
- **Output formats:** JSON and CSV (FlatJSON also available on Timeline LLX)
- **Geographic strength:** global, hyperlocal; history back to ~1970
- **Notable limitations:** billed per "record," and accounting differs by query type (a 15-day forecast is one record, but each historical record counts separately), which can surprise on bulk history; "Weather Data Provided by Visual Crossing" attribution is required on the free, Professional, and pay-as-you-go tiers (removed only at Corporate+); several business features — advanced solar/wind, soil & evapotranspiration, degree-day calculations, history-summary reporting, OData connector, external data sharing — are Corporate/Enterprise-only; Professional is single-user/single-concurrency; raw data may be shared within an organisation but not redistributed publicly; the Timeline LLX API is still in open beta
- **Built on:** Visual Crossing's proprietary "Weather Engine," which blends observations from 100,000+ surface stations, satellites, and high-resolution radar with government forecast models to answer past/present/future queries from a single database
