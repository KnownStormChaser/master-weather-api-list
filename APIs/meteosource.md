### Meteosource

Hyperlocal, ML-blended weather API from Czech provider Meteosource, combining many global models into a single bias-minimised forecast and layering regional models, station/AI adjustments, and real-time nowcasting on top — covering current conditions, minute/hourly/daily forecasts, historical, air quality, maritime, and map layers behind a clean self-serve tiered plan set.

- **Based in:** Czech Republic (operated by IFYS, Ltd; the data-protection notice is governed by the Czech Personal Data Protection Act and the site/pricing are Czech-localised — note: you'll see "UK" and "Slovakia" cited elsewhere because of the English "Ltd" naming and the Central-European base, but the registration and governing law are Czech)
- **Access model:** Instant self-serve — sign up with an email, get an API key; the Free, Flexi, Startup & Scale, and Standard plans are all self-serve. Enterprise (custom parameters/variables, 500k+ requests) is sales contact
- **Pricing model:** Freemium (ongoing free tier; self-serve paid tiers; Enterprise custom)
- **Coverage / data types:** current weather, minute-by-minute (1-hour) nowcast, hour-by-hour and daily forecasts (with text summaries), severe weather warnings, astronomy + climate normals, historical weather, maritime forecast, air-quality forecast, and weather map layers; bulk download on higher tiers; 15 languages on paid tiers (English only on Free)
- **Free tier:** ongoing — 400 calls/day, requires a "mention & backlink" attribution; 1-day forecast horizon, 7-day history, standard variables, English only, 10 calls/min, no SLA
- **Paid tiers (USD/month; billable also in CZK, GBP, EUR, PLN, ex-VAT):** Flexi $5 (100 calls/day then $1 per 100 extra; full 1-min/7-day forecast, 20-year history, full-resolution maps, 2,000/min); Startup & Scale $9.90 (20,000 calls/day, 10-min-step minute forecast, 2-day/10-day horizons, 7-day history, 100/min, 99.5% SLA); Standard $79 (400,000 calls/day, 1-min step, 4-day/30-day horizons, 1-year history + 0.5° maps + 5-day air quality, 250/min, 99.5% SLA; 10-day free trial); Enterprise custom / contact for pricing (custom parameters, 500k+/mo, volume discounts). Overage billed per 1,000 calls ($1, or $10/1,000 on Flexi)
- **Rate limits:** per-plan daily call quotas (400/day free → 400,000/day Standard) plus per-minute caps (10/min free, 100–250/min on mid-tiers, 2,000/min on Flexi); over-quota usage billed as overages; abuse/excessive use can suspend an account
- **Output formats:** JSON (REST); official wrapper libraries provided
- **Geographic strength:** global, hyperlocal by GPS coordinate; extra AI station-based adjustment in towns/cities and more populated areas; strong Central-European/Czech home market
- **Notable limitations:** the Free tier is narrow and requires public attribution (mention + backlink), with 1-day forecast, English-only, and 10 calls/min; forecast horizon, history depth (7 days → 20 years), minute-step resolution, languages, air quality, and maps are all tier-gated, and the tiers don't scale cleanly (e.g. cheap Flexi has full resolution/long history but a tiny daily quota, while mid-tiers trade those back for volume); JSON-only output; Enterprise/custom variables need sales contact; billing handled via Paddle as merchant of record
- **Built on:** Meteosource's own pipeline — a machine-learning blend of multiple global NWP models (trained on its archive of past forecasts/observations to minimise per-model bias, recomputed 4×/day), refined by in-house regional NWP models, per-city AI adjustments using station data, and real-time nowcasting from stations and radar; specific source models aren't publicly enumerated
- **Docs:** https://www.meteosource.com/documentation
