### The Weather Company

Enterprise weather data APIs from the company behind The Weather Channel and Weather Underground, spanning current conditions, forecasts, alerts, historical data, imagery, and a wide range of specialized feeds.

- **Based in:** Brookhaven, Georgia, USA
- **Access model:** Self-serve 30-day free trial (request form); Standard plan listed with an online "Buy now"; Enterprise via sales contact
- **Pricing model:** Paid (30-day free trial, no ongoing free tier)
- **Coverage / data types:** current conditions, daily/hourly/intraday forecasts (up to 15 days), severe weather alerts, historical, air quality, pollen, lifestyle indices, lightning, tropical, tides, aviation, agriculture, renewable energy, and weather imagery (radar/satellite)
- **Free tier:** none ongoing — 30-day trial only (50K API calls/day at 100 calls/minute)
- **Paid tiers:** Standard $500 USD/month (annual, 1 million API calls/month); Enterprise custom / contact for pricing
- **Rate limits:** trial capped at 100 calls/minute and 50K calls/day; Standard published as 1 million calls/month (per-minute limit not published)
- **Output formats:** JSON; imagery delivered as PNG tiles and raster/vector/gridded overlays
- **Geographic strength:** global, with especially deep US coverage
- **Notable limitations:** no ongoing free tier (trial only); Standard plan is an annual commitment; Enterprise pricing not public; major airlines must enroll in a custom Enterprise plan
- **Built on:** proprietary GRAF (Global High-Resolution Atmospheric Forecasting) model and the company's Forecast on Demand / Currents on Demand engines
- **Docs:** https://developer.weather.com/
- **Last verified:** 2026-06
