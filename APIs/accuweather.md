### AccuWeather

Global weather data APIs from one of the best-known forecasters, spanning current conditions, forecasts, minute-by-minute precipitation, alerts, indices, and imagery, with self-serve tiers that scale from hobbyist to high volume.

- **Based in:** State College, Pennsylvania, USA
- **Access model:** Instant self-serve (sign up at the developer portal, get a key); separate AccuWeather Enterprise APIs require sales contact (minimum 1-year contract)
- **Pricing model:** Paid (14-day free trial, no ongoing free tier)
- **Coverage / data types:** current conditions, daily & hourly forecasts, MinuteCast™ minute-by-minute precipitation, severe weather alerts, tropical, lifestyle indices, radar & satellite imagery, location services; limited historical (Daily Climate actuals/records/normals via Enterprise APIs only — no historical forecasts)
- **Free tier:** none ongoing — 14-day trial only (Core Weather 500 calls/day; MinuteCast™ 50 calls/day)
- **Paid tiers (Core Weather):** Starter $2/mo (15K calls/mo, personal/non-commercial only); Standard $25/mo (225K calls/mo); Prime $250/mo (1.8M calls/mo); Elite $500/mo (2.4M calls/mo, full suite). MinuteCast™ billed as a separate package (Starter $25/mo). Enterprise: custom / contact for pricing
- **Rate limits:** per-plan monthly call quotas; overage billed at per-plan CPM (cost-per-thousand) rates (e.g. $0.25 CPM above Starter's 15K, $0.12 CPM above Standard's 225K)
- **Output formats:** JSON (default, UTF-8); select APIs also support GeoJSON and CSV
- **Geographic strength:** global
- **Notable limitations:** Core Weather and MinuteCast™ are billed as separate packages; Starter plan is restricted to personal/non-commercial use; no historical forecasts, and Daily Climate data is Enterprise-only (1-year minimum contract); usage over plan limits incurs CPM overage charges; attribution/branding requirements apply per AccuWeather's brand guidelines
- **Docs:** https://developer.accuweather.com/
- **Last verified:** 2026-06
