### Meteomatics

Enterprise-grade weather, ocean, environment, and climate data API from Swiss firm Meteomatics, exposing 1,800+ parameters and 40+ models through a single parameterized-URL REST interface — covering real-time, forecast (to 15 days, extended to 46 days, seasonal to 7 months), historical back to 1940, and climate scenarios to 2100, all downscaled to 90 m. Access and pricing are sales-quoted.

- **Based in:** St. Gallen, Switzerland (Meteomatics AG; offices also in New York, Berlin, Oslo, Torquay, and Barcelona)
- **Access model:** Sales contact for the commercial Business Account — setup is "talk to an expert," no self-serve paid signup or public price list. A self-serve 14-day Business trial is available (request form), and a separate non-commercial Free Basic account also exists (see free tier)
- **Pricing model:** Custom / contact for pricing (industry-tailored packages, priced by capability and volume with volume discounts; no public numbers)
- **Coverage / data types:** point, multi-point, route (line), and polygon/area queries; deterministic and ensemble forecasts, real-time/current, historical (back to 1940 via ERA5), nowcasting, and climate projections to 2100; 1,800+ parameters spanning standard weather (temperature, precipitation, wind, pressure, humidity, cloud, radiation), marine/ocean (waves, currents, salinity, sea ice, tides), air quality / trace gases (CAMS), lightning, tropical cyclones, agriculture, soil, solar & wind power, satellite imagery, space weather, and weather warnings; also served via WMS/WFS and a vector tile server for map layers, plus the MetX visualization platform and a Weather Data Shop for one-time dataset purchases
- **Free tier:** no ongoing commercial free tier. A 14-day Business trial gives the full parameter set and model selection, 1,000 queries total (50/min, 10 parallel), with a subset of historical data on request. Separately, a non-commercial Free Basic account (private projects only) offers a 10-day hourly forecast for ~15 basic parameters
- **Paid tiers:** custom / contact for pricing (Business Account; capability- and volume-based packages by industry). One-off datasets can be bought à la carte through the Weather Data Shop
- **Rate limits:** plan-dependent and set per contract (the trial is capped at 1,000 queries, 50/min, 10 parallel); over-limit requests return HTTP 429. Global data typically available in the API within seconds of model release; updates as frequent as every minute for some parameters
- **Output formats:** JSON, CSV, XML, NetCDF, PNG, WebP, GeoTIFF, HTML; plus WMS/WFS and vector-tile outputs for GIS
- **Geographic strength:** global, downscaled to 90 m everywhere using NASA terrain data; uniquely high native resolution over Europe and the US via Meteomatics' proprietary 1 km EURO1k and US1k models (15-min steps, hourly updates)
- **Notable limitations:** no public pricing and no self-serve paid plan — commercial access requires a sales conversation; the generous trial is time-limited (14 days / 1,000 queries) and the only no-cost ongoing option is the non-commercial Free Basic account (private use, ~15 parameters); the breadth of 1,800+ parameters and 40+ selectable sources carries a learning curve; severe-weather alerting is offered but as a separate configurable product
- **Built on:** Meteomatics' "Mix" — an intelligent per-location, per-time blend of 40+ sources, all post-processed and downscaled to ~90 m. Global NWP: ECMWF IFS/ENS/VarEPS/seasonal, NOAA GFS/GEFS, DWD ICON, UK Met Office UM, Environment Canada GEM; AI models: ECMWF AIFS, FourCastNet, GraphCast; regional high-res: proprietary EURO1k and US1k, Météo-France AROME, DWD ICON-EU/ICON-D2, NOAA HRRR; plus CAMS and FMI SILAM (air quality), ECMWF WAM / Copernicus CMEMS / NOAA HYCOM (ocean), ERA5 and CHIRPS (reanalysis/rainfall), radar/satellite composites, station observations & MOS, and proprietary Meteodrone profiling data in select regions
